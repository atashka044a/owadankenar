Fresh Produce Logistics

Temperature-controlled export excellence

Owadan Kenar specializes in the international transportation and export of fresh fruits and vegetables — maintaining product quality from farm gate to final destination through fully monitored cold-chain logistics.

Our flagship offering is fresh tomato export: we handle sorting, packaging, refrigerated trucking, sea and air freight, and customs clearance for wholesalers, supermarkets, and distributors worldwide.

  • End-to-end cold chain from collection to delivery
  • Dedicated tomato export lanes to CIS, Middle East & Europe
  • B2B partnerships with retailers and food distributors
